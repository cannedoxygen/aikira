Aikira Concept

Constitutional AI Core

At the heart of Aikira is a smart contract integrated with an AI model. This AI has a charter of guiding principles that define its ideology – to maximize value, ensure fairness, and protect token holders.

Proposal & AI Deliberation

Community members submit proposals to the DAO, but instead of voting, they present arguments to the AI. The AI evaluates against its principles and available data before making decisions.

On-Chain Execution

Once the AI decides, smart contracts execute automatically. An oracle system allows the AI to sign off-chain decisions for on-chain execution with minimal human intervention.

Learning & Evolution

The AI governance improves itself through machine learning. It analyzes past decisions and outcomes, while token holders provide feedback to train the AI for better future decision-making.
