Airdrop Process

Submit Wallet Address

Enter your wallet address in the form above. Make sure it's a valid address compatible with the Base network.

Verification

Our system will verify your submission and add you to the distribution list based on your position in the queue.

Token Distribution

Once liquidity is added to exchanges, tokens will be automatically sent to your wallet within 24 hours.

Staking Opportunity

After receiving your tokens, you'll have the option to stake them to earn rewards and participate in governance.

Instant Distribution

Once liquidity is added to the exchange, eligible wallets will automatically receive their $AIKIRA tokens. No staking or claiming required.

No Lockups

All airdropped tokens are fully liquid from day one. There are no vesting periods or token lockups for airdrop participants.

Eligibility Criteria

Compatible Wallet: You must use a wallet that supports the Base network and ERC-20 tokens.

One Address Per Person: Multiple submissions from the same person will be filtered out.

Timing: Your position in the queue determines your tier. Once all 500 spots are filled, the airdrop closes.
