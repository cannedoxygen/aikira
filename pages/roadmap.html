Phase 1: Foundation (Q2-Q3 2025)

The initial phase focuses on establishing the core infrastructure and community. During this phase, we will:

  • Deploy token and smart contracts on Base network
  • Build initial community through airdrop and marketing
  • Implement basic AI governance functionality
  • Create educational content about AI governance
  • Set up initial liquidity pools and trading pairs

This phase sets the foundation for all future development and community growth.

Phase 2: Growth (Q4 2025-Q1 2026)

The growth phase expands the ecosystem and enhances the AI capabilities. Key activities include:

  • Launch the Aikira Grants program
  • Expand developer community with hackathons
  • Enhance AI decision-making algorithms
  • Establish strategic partnerships
  • Deploy cross-chain integration solutions

During this phase, we expect to see significant growth in community size and ecosystem development.

Phase 3: Maturity (Q2 2026 onwards)

The maturity phase focuses on long-term sustainability and advanced features:

  • Implement advanced AI governance with learning capabilities
  • Develop hybrid governance models
  • Create cross-DAO collaboration frameworks
  • Establish industry standards for AI governance
  • Achieve full decentralization of all protocol aspects

In this phase, Aikira aims to become an industry standard for AI-governed DAOs with a self-sustaining ecosystem.

Governance Evolution

The Aikira governance model will evolve through multiple iterations, with each phase introducing more complex decision-making capabilities to the AI and expanding the scope of what the DAO can accomplish.

Technical Framework

Our development focuses on creating a robust, transparent, and secure framework for AI governance that combines on-chain execution with off-chain intelligence processing.

Community Involvement

While the AI makes final decisions, community input remains essential for training, guidance, and domain expertise. Aikira aims to find the optimal balance between human input and AI autonomy.
